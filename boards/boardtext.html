Supported devices

Device drivers are included for:

  • Serial
  • Ethernet
  • FLASH
  • PCI
  • USB
  • PCMCIA/Compact Flash
  • Keypad
  • Touchscreen
  • Timekeeping (real-time clock)
  • Watchdog

Note that no proper device drivers are present in this port, although diagnostic output is available.
