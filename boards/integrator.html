ARM Integrator Development Board

A port of the ARM Integrator Development Board platform, based on the ARM7TDMI is available for both eCos and RedBoot.

The support for this platform was contributed to eCos, and Red Hat are grateful on behalf of the open source community to Philippe Robin at ARM. Note that this port has not been tested by Red Hat.

Supported devices

Device drivers are included for:

  • Serial
  • Ethernet
  • FLASH
  • PCI

Availability

Hardware

ARM Integrator boards may be purchased on-line here.

Software

This port is available in tarred gzip format here.

It is supplied as-is and was made against anonymous CVS as of the beginning of October 2000. It will inevitably require some merging to work against current anonymous CVS sources. There are some more comments from Philippe on the status as contributed in this readme.

When the port is finished, it will be integrated into the anonymous CVS repository in due course.

Documentation

Specifications are available for the processor and platform and are available on-line from the manufacturer's web site.

The most recent version of the full documentation set may be found on-line here.
