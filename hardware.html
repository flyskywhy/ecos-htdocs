Early access program

Support for the following additional hardware is available under an early access program:
  • Hitachi SH-3 processor and SH7708 evaluation board
  • NEC VR4300 processor and VRC4373 evaluation board
  • Intel SA-110 StrongARM processor and EBSA-285 evaluation board
  • Matsushita AM33 processor
  • Cirrus Logic EP7209 processor and EDB7209 development board
  • Cirrus Logic EP7211 processor and EDB7211 development board
  • Cirrus Logic EP7212 processor and EDB7212 development board
  • ARM9TDMI processor
  • ARM Thumb and Multi-ICE support
Contact Cygnus at ecos-info@cygnus.com for further information.
