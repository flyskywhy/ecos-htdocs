Submitting Patches to eCos

Revision 1.1

These pages describe how to prepare and submit a patch to eCos. While patches prepared and submitted in ways other than described by these guidelines will not necessarily be rejected out of hand, following these suggestions will greatly improve your chances of having a patch accepted.

The following pages describe in detail what is involved at each stage of writing, generating and submitting your change to eCos:
