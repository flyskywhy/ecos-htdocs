Keeping in Touch

Mailing lists

There are two principal mailing lists relating to eCos sourceware. Subscribers to the ecos-announce list will receive details of all eCos developments as they unfold. This is a moderated list intended principally for the announcement of new packages, snapshots and patches. An archive of this mailing list is available. To subscribe to ecos-announce, enter your e-mail address in the following box and click on the Subscribe button:
(ecos-announce)
Subscribers to the ecos-discuss list can participate in open discussion with others concerning all aspects of eCos. This is an unmoderated list. An archive of this mailing list is available. To subscribe to ecos-discuss, enter your e-mail address in the following box and click on the Subscribe button:
(ecos-discuss)
Further, more specialized mailing lists will be created and announced as the volume of traffic grows.

Change notification mailings lists

There is are additional mailing lists specifically for those requiring notification of changes to either eCos anonymous CVS repository or the eCos sourceware web site. Subscribers to the ecos-cvs list will receive immediate notification of any changes to the anonymous CVS repository. A digest version is also available where such changes are notified on a daily basis. To subscribe to ecos-cvs, enter your e-mail address in one of the following boxes and click on the associated Subscribe button:
(ecos-cvs)
(ecos-cvs-digest)
Subscribers to the ecos-webpages-cvs list will receive immediate notification of any changes to the eCos sourceware web pages. A digest version is also available where such changes are notified on a daily basis. To subscribe to ecos-webpages-cvs, enter your e-mail address in one of the following boxes and click on the associated Subscribe button:
(ecos-webpages-cvs)
(ecos-webpages-cvs-digest)

Contacting Cygnus

Private non-technical queries concerning the eCos product should be addressed to the eCos product team <ecos-info@cygnus.com>. Private technical queries concerning eCos sourceware development and collaboration should be addressed to the eCos maintainers <ecos-maintainers@cygnus.com>. Please note that the eCos maintainers are unable to provide technical support. Please use our general enquiries address <info@cygnus.com> for information concerning other Cygnus products.
