Keeping in Touch

Mailing lists

There are two mailing lists relating to eCos sourceware. Subscribers to the ecos-announce list will receive details of all eCos developments as they unfold. This is a moderated list intended principally for the announcement of new packages, snapshots and patches. To subscribe to ecos-announce, enter your e-mail address in the following box and click on the Subscribe button:
(ecos-announce)
Subscribers to the ecos-discuss list can participate in open discussion with others concerning all aspects of eCos. This is an unmoderated list. To subscribe to ecos-discuss, enter your e-mail address in the following box and click on the Subscribe button:
(ecos-discuss)
Further, more specialized mailing lists will be created and announced as the volume of traffic grows.

Contacting Cygnus

Private queries concerning eCos Sourceware development and collaboration should be addressed to the eCos maintainers <ecos-maintainers@cygnus.com>. Please note that the eCos maintainers are unable to provide technical support.
