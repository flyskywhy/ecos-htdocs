Revision 1.0

This table shows the copyright assignments made for eCos. For each assignment the table lists the name and contact details of the assigner, the date it was received, the body it was assigned to (Red Hat or the FSF), whether future work is also covered and a brief description of the assigned code.

Note that email addresses and company affiliations are given as they were at the time of the assignment. There is no guarantee that the contributor is still employed at the same company. If they are still active on the eCos mailing lists, it is worth checking a recent post for their current address.
